YouTube Topic Filter

Extract Quotes

Hover over any YouTube video thumbnail and click "Extract Quotes" to find all mentions of specific words or phrases in the video.

Note: For single words, the search matches whole words only. For phrases, it matches the exact phrase.

View Transcript

Hover over any YouTube video thumbnail and choose from two transcript options:

  • View Full Transcript - See the complete transcript with clickable timestamps
  • View Text Transcript - See just the text without timestamps

How to Use

  1. Hover over a YouTube video thumbnail
  2. Click one of the available buttons
  3. For quotes, enter the text you want to search for
  4. View all mentions with timestamps or the full transcript
  5. Click on timestamps to jump to that point in the video

Make sure the Flask server is running on port 9000.
